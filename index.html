The Midwest Sing & Stomp is an annual celebration of folk music and dance, highlighting the many vibrant traditions that have taken root in Chicago and the Great Lakes region. This project is partially supported by an Individual Artist Program Grant from the City of Chicago Department of Cultural Affairs & Special Events, as well as a grant from the Illinois Arts Council Agency, a state agency through federal funds provided by the National Endowment for the Arts.
