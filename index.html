About

The Midwest Sing & Stomp is a day-long festival of traditional folk music performances, workshops, and dancing from the Chicago area. From 2pm to midnight experience non-stop traditional programming on two stages: The Music Stage (upstairs) featuring Scottish, old time, English ballads, gospel, open jamming, and more; and the Dance Hall (downstairs) hosting shapenote singing, contra dancing, square dancing, and cajun two-stepping.
