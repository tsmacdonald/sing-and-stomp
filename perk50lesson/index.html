Private Music Lesson

We are offering a one-hour music lesson for you or someone you know in the Chicago area. This festival aims to entertain as well as expose people to folk music traditions, so in that spirit you will get a one-on-one lesson in guitar, fiddle, or cello for your $50 donation.

Lesson options include:

  • Guitar/fiddle/singing/songwriting with Spitzer Space Telescope
  • Fiddle with Tim Macdonald
  • Cello with Jeremy Ward
